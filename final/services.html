Model Train Maker

Exploring new technology

At Model Train Maker we offer many different services for your model railroading needs. We specialize in building one-off and small-run models of rare and unusual prototypes such as the Milwaukee Road SD10, The Galloping Goose, and the Double Fairlie from Britian. We also have an extensive library of parts that can be machined as is or modified to suit whatever your situation might be.

Our diverse tool range allows us to work in any scale from 1:220 all the way up to 1:3rd live steam! All of our products come with a 3 year warranty, and with our one-off models and prototypes we are able to offer a full refund within the first year for any mechanical or electrical defect on our part.

If models themselves aren't what you're after we are able to offer drafting and engineering services for all your wildest dreams. Want to own a miniature piece of American history? We can replicate the Jupiter or #119! Come and ask us about our diverse range of skills!
