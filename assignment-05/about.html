About John Steinbeck

John Ernst Steinbeck Jr. (1902-1968) was born in Salinas, California to parents
John Steinbeck Sr. and Olive Hamilton. After several years doing odd jobs, Steinbeck
published his first novel in 1929. From there, he went on to write many great works
with his most famous book, the Grapes of Wrath, being published in 1939. However,
other examples of his work include "Of Mice and Men" as well as "In Dubious Battle".
