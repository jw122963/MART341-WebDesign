A Quote from Harper Lee

"I wanted you to see what real courage is, instead of getting the idea that courage is a man with a gun in his hand. It’s when you know you’re licked before you begin but you begin anyway and you see it through no matter what. You rarely win, but sometimes you do.”

--Harper Lee, To Kill a Mockingbird, 1960

Nelle Harper Lee (1926-2016) was an American author best known for her novel, "To Kill a Mockingbird". The novel was loosely based around observations Lee made about events, family, and friends from her childhood. Her novel today is read by millions due to the perspective it brings about the irrationality of hatred, especially racism.
