Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • "Texas toast" bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Swiss cheese
  • Gouda cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Heat pan on stove using medium heat.
  2. Melt some butter in pan to grease it.
  3. Toast two slices of bread on both sides in the pand.
  4. Remove bread from pan and place on dinner plate.
  5. Stack cheese in any order, but with at least one slice of cheddar, swiss, gouda, and mozzarella.
  6. Add cheese stack to bread, sandwiching the cheese between the two toasted slices.
  7. Continue to cook sandwich in the pan, adjusting temperature as necessary.
  8. Flip sandwich with spatula after approximately 2 minutes.
  9. Continue cooking until the cheese is thoroghly melted all the way through the sandwich.
  10. Congradulations! You now have the cooking skills of a college student!
